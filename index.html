About Me

I grew up on the Navajo Nation in a small town on the southwest corner of the reservation. Leupp, Arizona is located about 50 miles northeast of Flagstaff, Arizona and 30 miles northwest of Winslow, Arizona. I attended Winslow High School, living in a dormitory for four years. Then after graduating from high school, I moved to Phoenix, Arizona to attend Arizona State University.

While still a student at ASU, I began working for the athletic department, specificially within their human resources office. I have worked for ASU for eighteen years. I suppose I stayed at ASU for so many years because I work with great people and have had excellent bosses. Not to mention, the perks are great, I get to attend football, basketball and baseball games for free. Working for a university also allowed me to complete my undergraduate degree in Interdisciplinary Studies (concentrations in Informatics and Business).

Although I do not have children of my own, I have three, beautiful grandchildren who are my world (in my culture they are referred to as my grandchildren but you would probably refer to them as my great-nieces). The oldest is nearly 3 years old, while the two younger ones are 11 months and 9 months old.
